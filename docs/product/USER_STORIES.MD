# KP Enterprise Software — User Stories

This document captures high-value user stories for the four core portals in KP Enterprise Software.

## 1. Public Site

The Public Site is the business-facing entry point for new visitors, prospects, and returning customers.

### Story 1: View services
**As a visitor, I want to browse the services offered by KP Enterprise so that I can understand whether the business can solve my problem.**

### Story 2: Submit an enquiry
**As a visitor, I want to submit a quote request or enquiry so that I can start the process of getting help with my project.**

### Story 3: See completed work
**As a visitor, I want to view completed projects and customer feedback so that I can judge the quality and professionalism of the business.**

---

## 2. Client Portal

The Client Portal gives customers controlled visibility into the progress of their own projects.

### Story 1: Track project progress
**As a customer, I want to see the status of my project so that I can know how the work is progressing without needing to visit the workshop.**

### Story 2: View updates and media
**As a customer, I want to view progress notes and uploaded photos so that I can stay informed and confident in the work being done.**

### Story 3: Leave feedback
**As a customer, I want to leave a review after project completion so that I can share my experience and help the business improve.**

---

## 3. Worker Portal

The Worker Portal supports welders, builders, and technicians in day-to-day execution.

### Story 1: View assigned jobs
**As a worker, I want to see the jobs assigned to me so that I know what tasks I need to complete today.**

### Story 2: Update task status
**As a worker, I want to mark tasks as in progress or complete so that the team can track the current state of the project.**

### Story 3: Upload progress evidence
**As a worker, I want to upload photos and work notes so that management and customers can see proof of progress.**

---

## 4. Admin Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard is used by the owner and operations staff to manage the business.

### Story 1: Convert enquiries into projects
**As an admin, I want to convert a customer enquiry into a project so that the business can begin structured delivery work.**

### Story 2: Assign workers to projects
**As an admin, I want to assign workers to projects and tasks so that the right people are allocated to the right jobs.**

### Story 3: Moderate customer reviews
**As an admin, I want to review and approve customer feedback so that only appropriate content is shown on the public website.**

---

## Notes on Story Design

These stories are intentionally written to support the MVP scope of KP Enterprise Software:

- public lead generation
- customer visibility
- worker execution
- administrative control

They are designed to be realistic, testable, and directly tied to business value.

## Future Expansion

Additional stories can be added later for:

- analytics and reporting
- automated notifications
- invoice and payment handling
- portfolio publishing
- advanced workflow rules
- multi-team scheduling
- third-party integrations
- mobile app features
This document will evolve as the product is developed and new user needs are discovered.
